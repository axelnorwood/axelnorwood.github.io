Writing Continuum

Track writing development across grades. Understand the writing traits, performance levels, and what "meeting the standard" looks like at each grade level (1–5).

Students develop foundational writing skills: clear ideas, simple sentences, and beginning to apply grammar and punctuation rules.

No clear, focused ideas
Approaching

Meeting
Develops clear, focused ideas
Indicators of meeting standard
  • Develops a clear main idea related to the task or topic
  • Includes details to support ideas
  • Maintains focus on the task

Indicators of meeting standard

  • Links ideas using basic connectives (e.g. and, but, because)
  • Writes sequences of sentences that flow logically
