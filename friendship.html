Making Friends 101

five easy steps to meet someone new

1
🎨 Do something interesting — OR find someone doing something interesting.
2
👋 Say Hi!
3
🧩 Let others join in — and find ways to contribute.
4
⏳ Give it time — friendships grow little by little.
5
🔁 Repeat! Keep being friendly and keep trying.

Resolving Conflict 101

when things go wrong, here's how to make them right

1
🤔 Think about how you might have made the problem worse — and say sorry if you did.
2
💬 Tell the other person how you felt when they did something you didn't like. Be specific!
3
🤝 Make a compromise — find something you can both agree on.
4
🌈 Let go of other people's mistakes — we all make them!
5
⭐ Be a good example: be caring, generous, and kind.

★ You've got this — be brave, be kind, be you ★
