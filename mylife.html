The Story of Me

Five quick stops to figure out who you are, and who you're becoming — one small step at a time.

📓

How this works

Grab a journal, notebook, or any paper and a pen you like. For each stop below, write your own answers in your journal. There are no wrong answers and no grades — the goal is just to think honestly and get to know yourself a little better. Take your time. You can do one stop a day if you want!

1

👀 Look Back

Think about the moments that made you, you — the big ones and the little ones.

In your journal, write about…
  • A happy memory you love
  • Something hard you got through
  • A moment you felt really proud
  • A mistake that taught you something
  • Someone who has helped you a lot
2

🪞 Look at Right Now

Be honest about the person you are today. This is just for you.

In your journal, write about…
  • Something you're good at
  • Something you find tricky
  • A habit that helps you
  • A habit you'd like to change
  • How you treat other people
3

🌱 Imagine Your Best Future

Picture yourself a few years from now if you keep trying your best. Dream big!

In your journal, write about…
  • The kind of person you want to become
  • A skill you'd love to learn
  • Something that would make you proud
  • The kind of friends you want
  • How you want your future life to feel
4

🧭 Spot the Wrong Turns

Every adventure has traps to avoid. What could pull you off the path you want?

In your journal, write about…
  • What might happen if you never practice
  • What might happen if you give up too fast
  • How being unkind could hurt you and others
  • A bad habit you'd like to avoid
  • A future that would make you unhappy
5

👣 Pick Your Tiny Steps

You don't need giant leaps — just small steps you can take again and again.

In your journal, write about…
  • One small habit that could help you grow
  • Something you could practice every day
  • A way you could help others more
  • A healthy choice you can repeat
  • One tiny step you can start today
★

✏️ You're All Set

When you finish, keep your journal somewhere safe. Come back in a few months and read it again — you'll be amazed how much you've grown.

There are no perfect answers. Just think honestly, write freely, and learn a little more about yourself.

Your future is built from small choices,
repeated over time. ♥
